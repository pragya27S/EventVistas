How do I access the faculty portal?

You can access the faculty portal by visiting the official website and clicking on "Faculty Login." Use your institutional email ID and password to log in. If you're facing any issues, contact the IT support team.

How can I submit attendance records?

Navigate to the "Attendance" section in the dashboard. Select your class and subject, mark attendance, and click "Submit." Make sure to submit within 24 hours of the class.
